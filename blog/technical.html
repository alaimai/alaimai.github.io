Lei's learning journey

a Dev academy web development project

Kia ora everyone, I'm Lei Zheng, please call me "Lay."

Welcome to my blog. I currently reside in Christchurch with my wife and two wonderful kids while juggling a full-time job.

Since my youth, I've been captivated by coding and harbored aspirations of becoming a software developer. However, my career took a different path upon moving to New Zealand, leading me to a physically demanding job.

Last year, I had an epiphany: my true passion lies in coding. This realization prompted me to enroll in Dev Academy, where I could reignite my love for coding and pursue a career aligned with my interests.

This blog marks my inaugural venture into self-built platforms, and I've relished every moment of its creation. From meticulous layout designs to experimenting with CSS properties, crafting this blog has been immensely enjoyable.

At its current stage, this blog remains a prototype, utilizing only HTML and CSS. I eagerly anticipate the next phase, where I'll delve into JavaScript during the upcoming sprint.

Thank you for joining me on this journey, and I look forward to sharing more updates with you soon.

Ciao
