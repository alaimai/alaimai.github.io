What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

Growth mindset: it is never too late to learn new thing, my abilities and intelligence can be developed through dedication and hard work. It is ok to ask for help when I am stuck. Cultivating emotional intelligence is essential in an individual's overall success and well-being.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Values shape the approach to programming and learning. Empathy allows me to understand and relate to the needs and perspectives of others, including users, teammate. Self-awareness involves understanding my own strengths, weaknesses, learning style and it enable me to understand when I need help.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

Before I understood what mindfulness and meditation really were, I had some biases against them because I thought they were connected to religion. But after learning that they're actually secular practices with proven benefits, my opinion changed. Now, I see them as useful tools for improving mental well-being, no matter your religious beliefs.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Core learning encompasses speaking and writing. My English proficiency poses a significant challenge, yet I view it as an opportunity for improvement.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

As programmers, we accomplish tasks using computers, but our primary interaction is with people. Effective communication and core learning aid us in this aspect.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

In Sprint 1, I initially focused on technical learning. However, as I progressed, I discovered that core learning surprised me more than technical learning.
